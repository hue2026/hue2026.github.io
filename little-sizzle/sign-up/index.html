Choose Your Username & Password
🤔 Think about it: Why should you NOT use your real name as your Scratch username? What could happen? Discuss with a partner before reading on.

✅ A GOOD Username:

Does NOT include your real name
Does NOT include your school or location
Is creative and easy to remember
Can include numbers or underscores
You'd be comfortable showing a teacher

❌ A BAD Username includes:

Your first + last name (e.g., JohnSmith)
Your school name or grade
Your phone number or birthday
Your address or neighborhood

Verify Your Email — The Final Step!
🤔 Before verifying: Can you still use Scratch without verifying your email? What features do you think would be unavailable? Make a prediction, then check the list below.

📬 How to Verify:

Open the email inbox you used to sign up
Look for an email from no-reply@scratch.mit.edu
Click the blue "Confirm my email address" link
You're returned to Scratch — account fully active! ✅

✅ Verification Unlocks:

Share your projects publicly
Comment on other people's projects
Follow other Scratchers
Password reset if you forget it
