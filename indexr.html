🔑 Key Vocabulary
😰 Competition Stress

The mental and physical tension that arises when we perceive a competitive situation as challenging or threatening.

🤝 Sportsmanship

Fair, respectful, and gracious behavior toward all participants — win or lose.

🌬️ Breathing Exercises

Deliberate breathing patterns (like box breathing) that calm the nervous system.

💬 Positive Self-Talk

Replacing negative inner dialogue with constructive, encouraging statements.

🧘 Mindfulness

Paying full attention to the present moment without judgment — staying grounded in the now.

⚡ Tilt

A state of emotional frustration that causes poor decision-making and impulsive play.

🧬 Understanding Competition Stress

What's actually happening in your body?

🧠 Fight-or-Flight Response: Your brain releases adrenaline and cortisol. Heart rate increases. Muscles tense. Your focus narrows — which can help or hurt your gameplay.

🤔 When might this stress response actually be useful in a match?

📈 The Yerkes-Dodson Curve: Some stress boosts performance — but too much causes sharp decline. Elite players learn to stay in the optimal zone.

Peak performance = moderate arousal, not zero stress.

🔑 Key insight: Stress isn't the enemy. Unmanaged stress is. The goal is regulation, not elimination.

🌬️ Breathing Techniques

Your built-in reset button

🟦 Box Breathing

In
Hold
Out
Hold

Inhale 4s → Hold 4s → Exhale 4s → Hold 4s. Repeat 4×. Used by Navy SEALs and elite athletes.

🟩 4-7-8 Breathing

🫁

Inhale 4s → Hold 7s → Exhale 8s. Activates the parasympathetic nervous system. Great between matches.

🟨 Tactical Breath

🎮

One deep exhale right after a goal is scored against you. Releases tension instantly. Use during replay screens.

🧘 Mindfulness in Gaming

Staying present when everything is chaos

Mindfulness = paying attention to the present moment on purpose, without judgment. In Rocket League, this means noticing the ball, your positioning, your breathing — not the mistake 30 seconds ago or the final score.

🧪 Quick mindfulness exercise:

  1. Hold your stress ball in both hands.
  2. Close your eyes for 30 seconds.
  3. Focus only on sensations: texture, temperature, weight.
  4. When your mind wanders, gently bring it back to the ball.

🤝 Defining Sportsmanship

More than just saying "gg"

✅ Sportsmanship Looks Like:

  • Complimenting an opponent on a great shot or save.
  • Encouraging a teammate after a mistake.
  • Accepting a loss graciously — no excuses, no blame.
  • Using quick chat constructively ("Nice shot!", "Close one!").
  • Owning your errors: "My bad."

❌ Poor Sportsmanship Looks Like:

  • Blaming teammates for every conceded goal.
  • Spamming "What a save!" sarcastically.
  • Going AFK or throwing when losing.
  • Trash-talking opponents in chat.
  • Refusing to acknowledge others' skill.
🤔 Think about it: Which type of player do you want to be known as? Which type would you rather have on your team? Why?
📊 The Ripple Effect

How sportsmanship shapes your entire experience

📉

On Poor Sportsmanship

Teams with toxic players lose more — not just because of skill, but because trust and coordination collapse. Teammates play scared, hesitant, and distracted.

📈

On Good Sportsmanship

Positive teams recover faster from deficits. Players take smarter risks. Morale stays high. Even losses feel like learning opportunities.

🔄

On You Personally

Your sportsmanship reputation follows you. Players remember who was respectful. Being a good teammate opens doors to better teams and communities.

🏆 Real talk:

Professional Rocket League players are known for their sportsmanship as much as their mechanics. Watch any RLCS Grand Final — the handshakes, the respect, the humility. That's not an accident; it's a standard.

🎮 Guided Practice · Gameplay Session

Applying techniques in real-time

📋 Instructions:

  1. Start a Rocket League match (casual or competitive — your choice).
  2. Before kickoff: Take one deep breath. Set an intention: "I will stay calm and respectful."
  3. During play: When you feel tension rising, apply at least one technique from your toolkit.
  4. After a goal (for or against): Use the replay to breathe. Reset. Refocus.
  5. Communicate: Use quick chat or voice positively. No sarcasm. No blame.

⏱️ First interval (10 min)

Play freely. Focus on noticing your stress signals without judging them.

⏱️ Second interval (10 min)

Actively apply techniques. After each goal, do a 2-second tactical exhale.
