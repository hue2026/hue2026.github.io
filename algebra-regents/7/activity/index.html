Part C — Vertex Form & Transformations

4 Problems · ~12 min

⚠️ Sign Trap Reminder
y = (x − 3)² has vertex x = +3.   y = (x + 3)² has vertex x = −3. The sign inside the parentheses is the OPPOSITE of h.

Part D — Real-World Projectile Motion

3 Problems · ~12 min

Height-time problems use h(t) = −16t² + bt + c (feet) or h(t) = −4.9t² + bt + c (meters). The vertex tells you peak time & max height.

Problem 17 — The Field Goal L3 · Spicy

A football is kicked from ground level. Its height in feet is modeled by h(t) = −16t² + 64t, where t is time in seconds.
(a) Identify a, b, c.   (b) At what time does the ball reach maximum height?   (c) What is the maximum height?   (d) When does the ball hit the ground?

(b) Peak time: sec   (c) Max height: ft
(d) Lands at t = sec
